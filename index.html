Jeevan

India's first whey protein based calf milk replacer

1 Kg jeevan milk replacer is equal to 10 Liter of milk.

Ingrediants udes: Sweet whey powder, Tosted soya Isolates, Double Refined palm oil, Essensital Minerals and vitamins.

Benefits:

1) Its economical compare to calves reared on natural milk

2) Decreased mortality rate in calves and sheeps.

3) Increased growth rate due to feeding of whey proteinbased milk replacer.

4) Decreased age of first calving by 4 to 5 months.
