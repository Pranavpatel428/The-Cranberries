About Econat

Econat Industries developed an organic insecticide ANIGUARD-O to prevent external parasites from livestock. Conventional farmers typically take care of the entire parasite problem in one step by using a pour-on inorganic pesticides, insecticidal dusts, insecticidal ear tags and feed additives. Organic farmers cannot use these synthetic solutions and must counter common parasite infestations through a combination of prevention and approved organic control. AniGuard-O is an organic solution to prevent external parasites from livestock. It is 100% natural, safe and non toxic product.

Ani Guard-o

"Ani Guard-O" to prevent the external parasites attack on live stocks. It is 100 % Organic, Safe and Non Toxic product and is a Natural Fine Powder material derived from Fossil Shell Flour, deadly to any insect and completely harmless to animals (Livestock)

How to use:

Most insects have a waxy outer shell covering their bodies. The microscopically sharp edges of Ani Guard-O contact the insect or parasite, and pierce their protective coating, so they soon dehydrate and die. The larvae are affected in the same way. This makes Ani Guard - O an excellent and totally natural control, with no indicating of mechanical or chemical damage to the animal tissue. Sprinkle small amount of Ani Guard–O powder on the affected area of livestock or Rub into the Coat of the Animals, where the area is damaged due to External Parasite Infestation.

Packing Available:

200 Gm & 5 Kg.

EndoHit (Oxfendazole)

EndoHit is an efficient feed containing Oxfendazole for dairy animals and small ruminants like sheep and goat.

Method of Feeding:

Mix with regular cattle feed in dry form and keep in front of the animal. Consult your veterinarian for assistance in the Dignosis, Treatment, and control of Parasitism.

Direction of use:

For Calves more than,

6 months and up to 15 months: 50gm daily for 2 consecutive days

For animals above 15 months/Adult animals: 100gm daily for 2 consecutive days
